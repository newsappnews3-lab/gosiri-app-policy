Privacy Policy – GOSIRI

Last updated: March 2025
App: GOSIRI – Dairy Farm Management
Developer / Publisher: GOSIRI

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly:

  • Phone number – for login and account verification
  • Name and dairy/farm name – when you register or set up your profile
  • App data – milk entries, income, expenditure, feed stock, livestock, product information, and related content you enter in the app

When you enable cloud sync, this data may also be stored on our servers to provide sync across devices.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the GOSIRI app
  • Authenticate you and manage your account
  • Sync your data across devices when you use cloud sync
  • Send reminders (e.g. milk entry, daily deduct) if you allow notifications
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

3. Data Storage and Security

  • On your device: Data may be stored locally (e.g. on the device storage).
  • In the cloud: If you use cloud sync, data may be stored on secure cloud infrastructure.

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Sharing of Information

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We may share data with service providers (e.g. cloud hosting, authentication) only as needed to operate the app.
  • We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, safety, or the safety of others.

5. Permissions

The app may request:

  • Camera – to capture photos for milk entries, products, livestock, etc.
  • Notifications – to send reminders (e.g. add milk, daily deduct).
  • Storage / Media – to save and display images.

You can revoke these permissions at any time in your device settings.

6. Your Choices

  • You can stop using the app at any time.
  • Local data can be cleared by uninstalling the app.
  • For cloud/account data, contact us to request account or data deletion.

7. Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via the app or by email where possible. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests (including data deletion), contact us:

  • Through the support or contact option in the app, or
  • newsappnews3@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy applies to the GOSIRI app available on Google Play and other distribution channels.
